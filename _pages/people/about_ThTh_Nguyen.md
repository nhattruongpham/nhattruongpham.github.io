Dr. <a href="https://sites.google.com/view/thanh-thi-nguyen">Thanh Thi Nguyen</a> received the Ph.D. degree in Mathematics and Statistics from <a href="https://www.monash.edu/">Monash University</a>, Clayton, VIC, Australia, in 2013. He was a Visiting Scholar with the <a href="https://www.cs.stanford.edu/">Department of Computer Science</a>, <a href="https://www.stanford.edu/">Stanford University</a>, Stanford, CA, USA, in 2015, and the <a href="https://edge.seas.harvard.edu/">Edge Computing Laboratory</a>, <a href="https://seas.harvard.edu/">John A. Paulson School of Engineering and Applied Sciences</a>, <a href="https://www.harvard.edu/">Harvard University</a>, Massachusetts, USA, in 2019. He is currently an Associate Professor (Research) at the <a href="https://research.monash.edu/en/organisations/department-of-data-science-ai">Department of Data Science & AI</a>, <a href="https://research.monash.edu/en/organisations/faculty-of-information-technology">Faculty of Information Technology</a>, <a href="https://www.monash.edu/">Monash University</a>, Clayton, VIC, Australia. He received an Alfred Deakin Post-Doctoral Research Fellowship in 2016, a European–Pacific Partnership for the ICT Expert Exchange Program Award from the European Commission in 2018, and an Australia–India Strategic Research Fund Early- and Mid-Career Fellowship from the Australian Academy of Science in 2020. He has expertise in various areas, including artificial intelligence, deep learning, deep reinforcement learning, cyber security, IoT, and data science.
