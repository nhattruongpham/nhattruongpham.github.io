Dr. <a href="https://mila.quebec/en/directory/linh-le">Linh Le</a> is an independent visiting researcher at <a href="https://www.uts.edu.au/">University of Technology Sydney (UTS)</a>, Australia. She received her PhD in <a href="https://eecs.uq.edu.au/">School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science</a> at <a href="https://www.uq.edu.au/">The University of Queensland (UQ)</a>, Australia. Her research interests include Natural Language Processing (NLP), Computer Vision (CV), signal processing, robotics, neurodegeneration detection with an emphasis on leveraging Machine Learning/ Deep Learning to enhance the performance of these systems. She has articles published in leading journals and conferences, including AAAI, NeurIPS, IJCAI. She is the frequent reviewer of leading journals and conferences, including NeurIPS, ICLR, AAMAS, CHI, CIKM, Nature. She has  teaching experience at UQ. The subjects taught are Machine Learning and AI.
