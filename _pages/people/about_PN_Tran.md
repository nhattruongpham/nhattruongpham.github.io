<a href="https://tpnam0901.github.io/">Phuong-Nam Tran</a> received his B.Sc. degree in Artificial Intelligence from FPT University, Vietnam, in 2023. From 2021 to 2023, he worked as a Junior AI Engineer at K&G company in Vietnam. During this time, he also served as an AI researcher at the startup Pythera AI, focusing on projects related to natural language processing and computer vision. Following this, he worked as a researcher at the <a href="https://aita-lab.github.io">AI Technology and Application Research Lab</a> at <a href="https://hcmuni.fpt.edu.vn/en-US/home">FPT University in Ho Chi Minh City</a>, Vietnam, under the supervision of Dr. <a href="https://dnmduc.github.io/">Duc Ngoc Minh Dang</a>. Currently, he is pursuing a Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering at <a href="https://www.khu.ac.kr/eng/user/main/view.do">Kyung Hee University</a> in the Republic of Korea and is a member of the <a href="http://networking.khu.ac.kr/">Networking Intelligence Laboratory</a>, led by Prof. <a href="http://networking.khu.ac.kr/bbs/board.php?bo_table=sub2_1">Choong Seon Hong</a>. His research interests include quantum machine learning, federated learning, image segmentation, multi-modal systems, and their applications in biometrics, audio & speech processing, and computer vision.
